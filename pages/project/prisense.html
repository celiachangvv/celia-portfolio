Prisense

UI/UX Design

Prisense provides small retail businesses (such as grocery stores) with the ability to check marketing prices. Users can access the latest pricing and average selling prices on Prisense, making their stores more competitive.

Proposal

Promo Website

Insight

Overview

Prisense is the second project of the third term at Langara College. We are required to collaborate with a team to complete a B2B website. In Vancouver, most grocery stores are affiliated with large corporations, making it challenging for small businesses to thrive. Therefore, we aim to provide a website to assist small enterprises in accessing the latest product prices, both highest and lowest, and understanding average prices. This can help them survive under the competition from large corporations.

My Team

The team is composed of 8 students, 4 designers, and 4 developers. I am the Designer Lead and a UI/UX designer. In my role as the Designer Lead, I assist other designers with their work and assign design tasks. I also ensure that I completed the primary conponents, such as the grid, and project proposal, presentation deck, and marketing asset. Additionally, I respect the opinions of my team members and seek their input.

Branding

The logo of Prisense features an eye, aligning with our core idea behind creating Prisense - "empowering users to have a clear understanding of the prices of all products" and "enabling users to track prices."
