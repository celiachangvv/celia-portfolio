Overview

Uniteer is the canstone project of the fourth term at Langara College. We are required to complete an application with each team. In Vancouver, most parents are working parents, which means they don't have much free time to enroll their children in extracurricular activities or volunteer opportunities. Considering the growth and safety of the children, we have decided to create an app for parents, organizations, and volunteers that allows them to easily address these issues.

My Team

The team is composed of 7 students, 4 designers, and 3 developers. I am the Designer Lead and a UI/UX designer. In my role as the Designer Lead, I assist other designers with their work and assign design tasks. I also ensure that I complete the primary content, such as the grid, before starting each task. Additionally, I respect the opinions of my team members and seek their input.

Branding

The logo of Uniteer draws inspiration from a little bird, symbolizing the boundless growth of children. The distinct colors of the wings and the body of the bird represent three key elements: parents, organizations, and volunteers. This signifies their collective commitment to safeguarding the growth of childrenâ€”each element is indispensable. The unique colors highlight the collaborative effort, emphasizing that parents, organizations, and volunteers work together harmoniously to nurture the unrestricted development of children.
