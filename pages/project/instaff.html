Instaff

UI/UX Design

Branding

Instaff can assist employees and employers in managing or applying for part-time or freelance jobs. Additionally, Instaff provides a review system to ensure a safe and secure working environment for both employees and employers.

Insight

Overview

Instaff is the first project of the second term at Langara College. We are required to collaborate with a team to complete a website, whether it is B2C or B2B. The concept behind this idea is that many international students are seeking casual work, but they are unsure where to find opportunities and how to manage job cancellations. Instaff can assist employees in applying for and contacting employers. Additionally, it incorporates a review system to ensure a safe working environment.

My Team

The team is composed of 7 students, 3 designers, and 4 developers. I am a UI/UX designer in this team. In my role as a UI/UX designer, I diligently follow the design tasks assigned by the Designer Lead, ensuring timely completion. I successfully handled user flow, wireframe, mockup, and prototype tasks. Furthermore, I value and respect the opinions of my team members, actively seeking their input.
