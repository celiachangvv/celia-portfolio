Insight

Overview

This calendar serves as more than just a timekeeping tool; it's a visual journey through our company's diverse venues. Beyond being a thoughtful gift for our employees, it extends its reach to other businesses, sharing the essence of our spaces. The design philosophy revolves around practicality and aesthetics. With numerous venues available for business rentals, each month unfolds with a vibrant display of our unique locations, accompanied by exclusive discounts. The enlarged daily squares cater to the practical needs of businesses, offering ample space for noting essential work details. Embracing tradition, I incorporated Chinese words representing each month, adding a touch of cultural richness to the calendar. It's not merely a desk accessory; it's a testament to the dynamic spirit and collaborative nature of our company, ready to accompany businesses through a year of productivity and success.

Design Concept

I created a 2022 calendar for my previous company, not only for our employees but also as a gift to other businesses. The design concept of this desk calendar is to showcase the company's venues. As there are many spaces available for business rentals, we provide discounts each month. Considering the business need to use the calendar for noting important work events, I enlarged the daily squares for practical use. I also added Chinese words indicating the months to give it a more traditional feel.
