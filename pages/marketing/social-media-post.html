Social Media Management

Description

My former employer was the Hong Kong co-working space Paragon Asia. Their mission is to assist and encourage all business owners as they grow their enterprises. During my 1.5 years there, I contributed to the company's social media following growth from 1,000 to 6,000 users (on Instagram). I have to assist with more than just social media management because the marketing department only has three employees, including the manager. Other tasks related to media include production, event planning, photography, and marketing. Because only two employees are typically in charge of all media-related work at this company, I have learned how to divide work and collaborate with other colleagues. As a result, we will carefully consider this when choosing the next project.

Post Created

Description

The main responsibility of this job is to create three posts per week on social media. Post content includes brand stories, business strategy, company news, and any company/clients/tenants promotions.

For the brand story and business strategy, I’ll do some research first and then start writing the article; once I’m done with the article, I’ll start designing the visuals for the post.

For company news or promotions, I'll get information from my manager or colleagues so I can start designing visuals and uploading to social media.
